Food In Korea

Spicy Cold Noodles
Bibim nengmyun is served in a stainless steel bowl with a cold broth, julienned cucumbers, Korean pear slices, boiled egg, and slice of cold boiled beef. The long and thing noodles are ,ade from flour and buckwheat or sweet potatoes, through seaweed and green tea are also used for other variations.

Tteobokki
Tteokbokki consists of cylindrical rice cakes drenched in a sweet red chili sauce. Often accompanied by rice cakes and garnished with green onions, this popular street food dish is spicy and unforgettable. Tteokbokki originated during the Joseon Dynasty period and was originally a braised dish of sliced rice cakes, meat, eggs and Korean spices.
